About Me

Click Here to View my Resume

My name is Stefan Tomovski, I am currently a senior in information technology. I am currently attaining my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the University of Missouri. I am also achieving my web development certificate along with my cybersecurity certificate and my digital media design certificate. I needed work experience so in my sophomore year I picked up an IT support/technician student job at the University of Missouri’s School of Medicine.
The job and the experience I have attained while working there have been enlightening in teaching me what the future of information technology may hold. In high school I didn’t have much of an idea what I was going to do for school, so I went into IT, and it turned out to be a perfect fit where I have passion. A goal I have to complete by the end of this summer is to attain my CompTIA certification for networking and cybersecurity. As Information Technology is a very broad field of study, my specific interest is cybersecurity.
